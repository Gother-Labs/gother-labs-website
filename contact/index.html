Private collaboration

We are open to private conversations around important kernels, governed improvement, and systems that deserve more rigor than they usually receive. If there is a real operational surface behind the conversation, that is enough to begin. We prefer substance over ceremony and would rather start from the problem than from a polished pitch.

Research and partnership

If you want to discuss research, a partnership, or a kernel that deserves serious evaluation, we would rather begin from the technical and operational reality than from an abstract thesis. The most useful conversations start with the system itself, the constraints around it, and why better judgment would matter.

Careers

If you think you are unusually aligned with how we work, write to us. Relevant context, work samples, or a concise case for fit is more useful than a formal application. We care more about judgment, rigor, and operating taste than about volume, pedigree, or process theater.
