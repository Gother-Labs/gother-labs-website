What we do

We turn optimization debt into governed progress.

Better policies and heuristics

We work on systems that make repeated decisions: routing, planning, ranking, pricing, allocation, and related operating policies.

Better code under constraints

We search for stronger implementations without losing traceability, reproducibility, or confidence in behavior.

Better improvement loops
