Evaluation-driven optimization

We build governed improvement loops for high-impact algorithms: measurable, reproducible, and designed for real constraints.

What Göther is

Göther Labs is a research-driven team building systems that search for better policies, heuristics, and code under an executable definition of success. We focus on the gap between “good-enough legacy behavior” and what is achievable under today’s constraints.

How we work

Every effort starts by defining an evaluator: a score, non-negotiable constraints, tests, and a controlled compute budget. Candidates are generated, evaluated, logged, and gated. Only robust improvements survive.

Why this matters

Many organizations carry optimization debt: key algorithms and operating policies drift, get patched by hand, and stop being re-tested. A governed improvement loop turns that hidden cost into measurable progress without hype, and without unsafe rollouts.
