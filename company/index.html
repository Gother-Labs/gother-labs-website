Company

Göther builds governed search systems for important algorithms.

We are interested in the gap between acceptable legacy behavior and what is actually achievable when a kernel is evaluated seriously.

What Göther is

Göther Labs is a research-driven team building systems that search for better policies, heuristics, and code under an executable definition of success.

What we optimize for

We prefer bounded kernels, frozen baselines, explicit constraints, and runs that leave behind artifacts. Better should be inspectable, not rhetorical.

Why this matters

Many organizations carry optimization debt: important algorithms drift, get patched locally, and stop being seriously re-evaluated. A governed loop turns that drift into a disciplined search problem.
